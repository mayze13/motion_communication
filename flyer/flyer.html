Join the Minds in Motion study

Participate in cutting edge scientific research

What does space do to the mind? Help UCL researchers find out inside a unique 40,000 m² state-of-the-art research facility.

Led by Prof. Hugo Spiers (UCL Experimental Psychology), Dr Fiona Zisch (UCL Bartlett School of Architecture) and James Moultrie (PhD student, UCL)
  • Explore purpose-built architectural spaces at UCL PEARL in Dagenham, East London
  • Wear EEG headsets, eye trackers, and biosensors, all fitted by the research team
  • One hour in total. No specialist knowledge needed. All adults aged 18 and over welcome.
  • Your data contributes directly to how hospitals, schools, and offices will be designed
✦  Follow-up to 100 Minds in Motion (Sept 2024), as featured by MIT Technology Review, UCL News and Autodesk Research
